How It Works

From Idea
to Installation

Step 01

Initial Conversation

We discuss your ideas, the space, your design vision, and your budget. No obligation — just a relaxed, open conversation about what's possible.

Step 02

Design & Planning

I develop a tailored concept for your project, carefully selecting textures, finishes, and forms that complement your property and surroundings.

Step 03

Handcrafted Build

Every surface is hand-carved on site without moulds or stamps. The work is precise, unhurried, and built to endure the British climate for decades.

Step 04

Final Finish

Durable, weather-resistant finishes are applied and the site is left clean and complete. A feature that will define your outdoor space for years to come.
